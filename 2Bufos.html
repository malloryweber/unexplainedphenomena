Unidentified Flying Objects
by the Mini Marshmallows

Are UFOs real?

Yes, because they can literally be anything unidentified flying in the air!

What's inside UFOs?

Since we don't know what UFOs are, there could be aliens, space gadgets, air, or anything else.

Where do they come from?

They might come from anywhere - could just be something like a balloon in the fog.

What do people mistake for UFOs?

Airplanes, weather balloons, planets, stars, and birds are commonly mistaken for alien spaceships.

How many UFO sightings per year?

About 200 per month, which means around 2,400 sightings every year!

Are UFOs dangerous?

In theory, UFOs could be dangerous someday if aliens come, but no one has ever been hurt by aliens.
