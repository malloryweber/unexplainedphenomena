The Bermuda Triangle

By the Mini Marshmallows

Is the Bermuda Triangle actually a place?
Yes! The Bermuda Triangle is a real place in the ocean.
Does it look like a triangle?
It looks like a triangle on a map, but it doesn't look like a triangle when you're there.
Is it cold or hot?
It's hot because it's connected to Florida and the Caribbean.
Is it even a triangle?
It could just be a name. Not everyone agrees on the boundaries.
How big is it?
About 440,000 square miles - that's really big!
How many sides does it have?
3 sides, but they're invisible borders in the ocean.
Why does stuff disappear into it?
Possibly because of storms and big waves called "rogue waves". But things don't go missing in the Bermuda Triangle more than other similar places.
Is there even water in the Bermuda Triangle?
Yes, it's in the ocean!
How many boats have gone missing?
About 50 boats have gone missing there.
When was the Bermuda Triangle first noticed?
It wasn't built, but people started telling stories about it around the 1850s or later.
When boats vanish, where do they end up?
They probably get shipwrecked or sink to the bottom of the ocean.
