Q&A by the 2A Dancing Donuts - Glazed

1
What is a UFO?
Unidentified Flying Objects, or something you see in the sky but you don't know what it is.
2
Are UFOs real?
Yes, because we see things in the sky that we can't explain - but they can be anything, they don't have to be aliens. For example, they can be a balloon.
3
Why do we assume UFOs come from aliens?
Because what else would they come from? They look so different from anything humans have ever created, and we imagine that only aliens could fit in them.
4
Are alien worlds real?
Possibly, and possibly not, because we don't have any proof that aliens are real.
